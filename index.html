Crystals Collector
Have fun with crystals!

Instructions

You will be shown a random number.
When you click on a crystal, it will add a specific amount of points to your total.
You win if your total matches the random number.
You lose if your total goes above the random number.
The game restarts if you win or lose.
When the game begins again, you will see a new random number and all crystals will have a new amount of points.
